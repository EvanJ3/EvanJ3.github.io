Current Projects

Cloning Key Fobs with HackRF

If you've ever lost one of these fobs, you certainly know the pain of dealer reprogramming fees. In this project I use the Hack RF One SDR to isolate, solve, and replicate my personal vehicle's rolling code. Then adding the program and frequency to a blank fob.

Fama & French Factor Models from Scratch

As part of my ongoing work as a TA for MGT 6203 (Business Analytics) at Georiga Tech, I've been working on a scratch R implementation of 5 factor model using juypyter notebooks to help students better understand the technical subtleties and methods used to generate this popular financial model covered in the course.

Deep Dub

The goal of this project is to leverage the properies of generative adversarial neural networks in conjunction with langague transformers to create automated dubbing in animated video in non-source languages. The premise of the project rests upon leveraging the ample avaliablity of training data in existing dubbed animated shows in a variety of languages to train our language tranformers and GAN's to output reasonable dubs for out of sample animation projects with different target lanaguge from source.

Bridging The Gap

This application, developed by Team054 for the Georgia Institute of Technology's CSE 6242: Data and Visual Analytics course, is a modern adaptation of existing tools for understanding and visualizing bridge infrastructure data in the United States. There are over 617,000 bridges in the country, all of them critical to the transporation of goods, services, and individuals. This application allows anyone to view essential details about each of these critical pieces of infrastructure. Use the navigation bar or links below to explore our interactive dashboards, modeling information, and to learn various information about the project.

Yagi 360

The yagi antenna is great for direction signal detection. However, by using some 3D printed gears, some housing, a couple of actuators, and an arduino we create a yagi capable of 360 degree rotation and 180 degree tilt all controlled with an xbox remote.

Big Brother Oracle

Big Brother is a popular reality show on CBS where contestants live in a house isolated entirely from the outside world for a chance to win a half-million dollar grand prize. In this project, I collected data from all seasons and all previous players in an effort to predict winning probablities for each player on a rolling basis as the game progresses.

Mandelbrot in JS
