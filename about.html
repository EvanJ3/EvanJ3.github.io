About Me

Hi, my name is Evan Jones, but everyone calls me EJ. I am a machine learning engineer and data scientist. I currently serve as a lead Graduate Teaching Assistant at Georgia Tech serving under Dr. Frederic Bien. Our course, Analytics in Business, is core course in Georgia Tech’s MS of Analytics program and covers a wide variety of cross cutting topics from logistics, marketing, finance, and each of their connections to data analytics. I graduated from Georgia Tech’s MS in Analytics with a concentration in computational statistics in the fall of 2021. Prior to earning my MS, I obtained my BBA in Finance with a specialization in asset and security valuation from the University of Georgia in the Spring of 2019. At present time, I am seeking a full-time position in either a machine learning engineer or data analyst role. If you are an employer and think I would be a good fit for your open position, feel free to reach out.

Bloomberg Markets

Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC) is a 10-hour course that provides an interactive introduction to the financial markets using Bloomberg Terminals. BMC consists of 6 sections, Core Concepts, Economic Indicators, Currencies, Fixed Income, Equities, and Portfolio Management. The sections are woven together from Bloomberg data, news, analytics, and television.

Factset Core

A four-hour course taught by FactSet on the utilization of the FactSet technology platform and product suite. Topics covered include Navigating FactSet, Staying on Top of the Markets, Equities, and Fixed Income. The program was designed to demonstrate an individual has attained a certain level of expertise with different FactSet products across multiple asset classes.

Factset Portfolio Analytics

A four-part course with a comprehensive exam covering the process of portfolio analysis utilizing Factset's product suite. Particular focus is paid to performance attribution as well as decision making based upon underlying analysis.
