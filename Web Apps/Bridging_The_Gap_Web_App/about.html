About our Project...

Within the United States, there are more than 617,000 bridges each of which is paramount to ensuring the safe and reliable transit of goods, services, and individuals throughout the country. Despite their critical function, a significant proportion of the nation's bridges are in a state of disrepair, creating an issue of substantial public concern. However, this issue is not often recognized in part due to the lack of non-proprietary solutions for transparent exploration and visualization of the topic. Today's most recognized tool to explore bridge data, the "InfoBridge" web portal, lacks the clarity and flexibility expected of modern web applications. We developed this application to fill this gap in technology, seeking to build a tool that is not only informative, but open-source and easy-to-use. We hope you enjoy it.
