Data Analytics in Business

Data Analytics for Business is one of Georgia Tech’s core courses within the MS in Analytics program. In addition to Georgia Tech Masters students, we also provide a similar course experience through Georgia Tech & Edx’s Micro-masters in Analytics Program offered to individuals outside of Georgia Tech. The course which is offered each semester covers a wide variety of cross-cutting industry topics such as marketing, finance, logistics, and how each can be improved with analytical methods. The course serves as a survey of the masters and micro-masters programs as a whole, allowing students to get exposure to a breadth of analytical methods to better inform their elective choices later on in the program, and which courses are most relevant to their industry needs. In true Inter-disciplinary fashion, the course is taught by four of Georgia Tech’s best professor each covering the sections within the course relevant to their area of expertise. Dr. Narasimhan covers concepts in statistics and regression, Dr. Myers covers the units on logistics and supply chain management, Dr. Bien lectures on digital marketing, and Dr. Clarke covers analytical methods in finance.
